Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Total Flights The total number of scheduled flights recorded in the dataset.
Average Delay Aggregated average of delay minutes per flight.
Total Cancellations Count of flights that did not operate as scheduled.
On-Time Performance Percentage of flights arriving within 15 minutes of scheduled time.
Severe Disruptions Percentage of flights experiencing extreme delays or cancellations.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does the "On-Time Performance" represent?
On-Time Performance is the percentage of flights that depart or arrive within 15 minutes of their scheduled time, which is the standard industry metric for punctuality.
2. How are delay causes categorized?
Delays are categorized into Carrier (airline-controlled issues), Weather, NAS (National Airspace System/ATC constraints), Security, and Late Aircraft (cascading delays from previous legs).
3. What is the source of this aviation data?
The current dataset is derived from the Kaggle Airlines Flights Data, containing detailed flight logs from major carriers across numerous airports.
